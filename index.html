What is ... slightly opaque?

slightly opaque is the homepage of Caroline Kikisch who does several things including writing, illustrating and hacking.
I work freelance as a cultural anthropologist / sociologist and as a consultant in IT (e.g. quality assurance and project management).

For more information about what I do and what I am interested in, please see the About section.

If you'd rather enjoy some writing, please visit Articles for non-fictional texts (and rants) or meet Santa, a slightly atypical character.
Occasionally, I write about Hacking and provide the links to the Github repository where you can find the scripts and small programs I wrote.
Enjoy!
